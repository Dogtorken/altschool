Reason for choosing AltSchool Africa

I decided to enroll in AltSchool Africa's software engineering program for several reasons. First and foremost, the fully online and self-paced nature of their courses appealed to me. As a veterinarian working a demanding 9-5 job, flexibility is crucial for me. AltSchool Africa's approach allows me to learn at my own pace, fitting the coursework into my busy schedule without the constraints of traditional classroom settings.

Being a beginner in tech, I appreciated the beginner-friendly nature of AltSchool Africa's courses. The program is designed to cater to individuals like me who may not have a background in computer science or technology. The curriculum is structured in a way that breaks down complex concepts into easily understandable modules, making it accessible for someone starting from scratch.

One of the key benefits I expect from this program is the opportunity to acquire new skills in software engineering. As the tech industry continues to evolve, I recognize the importance of staying updated with the latest technological advancements. AltSchool Africa's program offers a practical and hands-on approach, ensuring that I not only grasp theoretical knowledge but also gain the skills necessary to apply that knowledge in real-world scenarios.

Moreover, I am excited about the prospect of expanding my career horizons. With a background in veterinary medicine, venturing into software engineering opens up new possibilities for me. The demand for tech professionals is widespread, and I believe that acquiring these skills will provide me with a valuable edge in the job market.

Additionally, AltSchool Africa's community and support system are crucial for someone like me transitioning into a completely different field. The ability to connect with instructors and fellow learners online fosters a collaborative learning environment. I am looking forward to engaging with a community of like-minded individuals, sharing experiences, and receiving guidance as I navigate through the challenges of learning a new skill set.

In summary, I chose AltSchool Africa for its flexible, beginner-friendly, and practical approach to software engineering education. I anticipate gaining valuable skills, expanding my career options, and building a supportive network as I embark on this exciting journey into the world of technology.
